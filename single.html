IT IS ALRIGHT TO BE SINGLE!!!!!!

I know exactly how enticing the idea of a first boyfriend, first date, and first kiss sounds. But let me tell you, IT IS ALRIGHT TO BE SINGLE!!!!!! I got through my teens without any of the aforementioned and trust me, it is not as bad as people make it out to be. Initially, this was going to be just a single blog, but I had too many points so had to split it into the positive (The upside to being single!) and negative aspects. Let me begin with the lows.

#1. Matchmaking

It is no hidden fact that peer-pressure is certainly a reason you WILL feel upset. Your friends become your cupids. And they are, without a doubt, better than Sima Taparia when it comes to matchmaking (we’re BFF’s if you get my reference xD). And, at times you feel pressured to get into a relationship yet, you have no prospective other.

And it is NOT your fault. Maybe you did not find what you are looking for or you are driven to find something else. Maybe your crush turned out to be a moron and you just gave up on the idea of crushes! Or you have seen relationships that ended miserably and you just do not want to get into one. Regardless, it is YOUR choice. YOU CAN BE SINGLE. Even though your friends think you desperately need a relationship.

#2. Jealousy

Oh, let’s not forget the green-eyed monster. Jealousy!! Honestly, I’ve been there. I’ve seen my best friends getting into their first relationship, all giddy and feeling all those said ‘butterflies in the stomach’. And yes, you will feel the need to have all of that and more and it is completely okay! Hell, I still crib to my friends about being single when I come across any cute post on Instagram which is definitely more than what I’d like to admit.

Again, it is completely fine. You do not have to rush into finding someone who will give you all of that. You cannot even be certain if someone will give you that. Just wait it out. Do not let this pressure you into getting into a relationship.

#3. Esteem

The final aspect I would address under the long list of “upsetting things” is something I see happening more often than anything else. Self-doubt. It SUCKS! You feel ugly and worthless. You conclude that there is something wrong with you that is why the person you like does not like you back. Or you feel extremely invalidated and think you will never find anyone to love you because you are not worth loving. JUST STOP.

Never hit this phase and if you do reach this, look into the mirror and say ‘I love you’. Everyone craves love and it comes in all kinds. Platonic, Familial, and the most important kind, SELF-LOVE. Do not underestimate the potential. Your beauty and worth are beyond your understanding. And any time you feel anything less, remember there are people who want to be half as amazing as you! Reach out to the people who love you and bask in the glory of you! Do not let a status like being ‘single’ determine your worth.

“Everything happens for a reason”, is a phrase I strongly believe in. For better or for worse ( I know, sounds so much like a vow xD) you will DEFINITELY have yourself. In the future, someone may join to cherish you and love you but until then remember to keep loving you for who you are, and again, IT IS ABSO-FREAKING-LUTELY OKAY TO BE SINGLE!

These are certain things I wish I was told rather than the ones you find online which are extremely conflicting. I hope you find the answers you are looking for! Oh and keep an eye out for part II which is filled with all the bonuses you get from being single!

Until then
Love
M

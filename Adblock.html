HOW WILL THIS UNFOLD

Words once said cannot be taken back but it can be contained and the failure to do so has drastically reduced the amount of money Facebook received from companies. Facebook and Instagram are two widely used social media platforms and in recent times, have seen the bitter side to frustration. With everyone jumping over to these modes to leave their comments or issues online, these sites have seen an increase in the amount of hate speech that is being put up on their apps. But, it is the lack of action from their side that has led to this woe that they face now.

Initially, it seemed like a small network of companies coming together to boycott their ad spending which was displayed along with hate speech. But, it became a huge ordeal when Verizon, the first big name in this fiasco, pulled out its ad spending from Facebook and Instagram.

But, this was just the beginning of the trials Facebook and Instagram faced because very recently, Microsoft, the third-largest investor in ad spending for Facebook boycotted all the funds, at least till August, for the same reason. This shook the base as Facebook reportedly lost over 60 billion dollars.

And, there seems to be no end with major advertisers halting or totally suspending their funds. Facebook and Instagram under Facebook are now under the major tribulation. Hate speech moderation, in recent times, is a must. And not doing their part in this, effectively has led to a huge loss both financially and morally for a company which has and is witnessing a high amount of netizen traffic.

When it comes to the tech giant, Microsoft, this action did not come off as much of a surprise as earlier this year, Microsoft reportedly suspended most of its ad spending. While advertisements have been a great marketing trick to get their product across, during recent times, excessive advertisements have changed this field.

Further, with the advent of ad blockers and them being highly and easily accessible, the whole purpose of spending on advertisements seems to have been defeated. But, that is not the pressing issue here. The fact remains that hate speech needs to be contained.

“With great power, comes great responsibility”

And, it is the responsibility of not just Facebook and Instagram but every platform that exists online to make sure that the hate, inappropriate content is moderated. It is no secret that the internet is the point of convergence of all walks and sets of people. Without saying, it becomes the responsibility of the ones in the power to make sure it is a safe place and while safety cannot be guaranteed to 100%, taking minimal steps in attaining this safe place, would be great for both the creators and users.

Personally, I feel like this is a long-overdue step when it comes to ensuring the standard of an online environment is maintained. This will make everyone reconsider what is their approach and how it can be better so as to not face any loss monetarily. This is a lesson that is sure to bring about changes in terms of moderation of content.

APPLE STORE IN INDIA

Home Professional Apple store in India

Apple has now finally launched its online store in India!

The American multinational technology company added India to its e-commerce platform as the 38th country. Until now, Apple depended on third-party companies like Flipkart and Amazon to deliver its products to the patrons.

From 23rd September 2020, all Apple products will now be available on apple.com/in with various additional bonuses. And this is a smart move for Apple in various aspects, especially since the promotion of the Make in India initiative. And the customers couldn’t be more thrilled as this change will now bring about various benefits to the buyer!

While Apple has always been a big name across the world, its online stores were limited. In fact, it wasn’t until very recently that the establishment decided to produce its products in India, the world’s second-largest smartphone market. The company now locally assembles the latest generation of phones and other products.

This transition allows Apple to lower the prices that it charged additionally, concerning custom duty taxes. Along with this, the firm has taken into consideration how the regional languages vary and the site offers support in both Hindi and English.

Similar to the online stores that exist in the other 37 countries, apple.com/in will also let you contact an Apple specialist for support on any product. Further, with the launch of this site, the whole range of products will now be available on the site ranging from HomePod to MacBook.

And this move is deemed to be strategic as this site is launched just before Diwali, a festive season where the market soars with new purchases. In fact, Apple is offering a set of customization like engravings and more on products like Apple AirPods, Apple iPad, and the Apple Pencil, ahead of this festive season.

Further, with the introduction of this online store, trade-ins will increase. There is a possibility of getting up to 35,000 rupees off the desired iPhone in exchange, provided a few questions are answered. But, this will definitely see a rise in the customers, as these trade-ins will help reduce the cost of the iPhone

Similar to the stores in other countries, the company now allows users to customize their iMac, MacBook Air, Mac Mini, and other Mac computers. The establishment has also initiated the student discount which will be provided on products across the store. They can also avail reductions in the prices of various other accessories and on AppleCare+, the extended warranty program.

In order to deliver the products to the buyers, Apple has partnered with Blue Dart, an Indian logistics company providing courier delivery services. And in light of the recent events, they will now be serving a contactless delivery. The shipping will be free of cost, countrywide, and will deliver within 24-72 hours of payment.

Staying on the topic of payment, this e-commerce platform will accept multiple modes of payment like credit and debit cards, credit card EMI, UPI, RuPay along with net banking, and card on delivery option. With the upcoming sale seasons due to festivities and the expected unveiling of the new generation of iPhones, this seems to be the perfect time to launch a store. This will certainly boost the sale of this tech giant.

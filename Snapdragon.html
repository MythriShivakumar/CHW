Snapdragon 865+ is lightning, indeed!

Ever since the introduction of smartphones, regular updates leave people craving for more. But, Qualcomm has introduced what is the dream of any android user, the Snapdragon 865+. This is the golden chip for people of all walks. The features in this tiny packet of joy is leaving every tech-savvy person, drooling after it.

Qualcomm has truly outdone itself with the introduction of Snapdragon 865+. It is one step ahead of the Snapdragon 865, which is a whole new generation in terms of processors. Snapdragon 865+ has broken barriers in terms of speed, hitting the 3.1Ghz mark, which is 10% higher than its predecessors.

It was not long ago when ASUS produced a stellar phone meant for gaming, where Snapdragon 855+ was introduced. And, it suffices to say, it took the field by storm with its beyond brilliant features and now? With the introduction of Snapdragon 865+, which ASUS will be using in its latest phone, the ROG3, and it is sure to be miles ahead in terms of gaming. The speed will be unparalleled by any other android.

With 5G being the latest change in terms of network and connectivity, the Snapdragon 865+ ensures it will work along with it. Further, the modem RF system will enhance the gaming aspects. It also improves the graphics from the before versions by 10%! And while 10% may not seem huge, it is to be kept in mind, that even the versions before 865+ had impeccable graphics.

Moreover, Qualcomm promises that Snapdragon 865+ is packed with features that will deliver gaming experience like that of the ones on the desktop. And how is this possible? Because for the first time ever, there will be an updateable GPU driver along with 5G gameplay, and what is jawdropping is the speed of 144fps!

And thatâ€™s not it. No, there is more. Yes, this small chip has more staggering features! You will be able to witness 5G gameplay and it will certainly be ultrasmooth. It also includes a True 10-bit HDR display which will ensure that your view will not defer much from that of the cinematic detail. Additionally, this chip offers WiFi up to a speed of 3.6Gbps. Yep, you read it right. It is now the fastest of any other WiFi provisions offered in the industry.

As mentioned prior, ASUS will be using the Snapdragon 865+ in the next generation ROG phone 3, Lenovo will also be using it in its latest Lenovo Legion which will be a mobile version of the Lenovo Legion which was launched three years ago. With big names chasing this new processor, the future of commercial use of this chip is bright. We will have to wait until the third quarter of 2020, to know more about its commercial use.

In a nutshell, the snapdragon 865+ has out clocked any of the past processors and is paving a path of its own. And, this path is going to be a whole new revolution in terms of speed and graphics. It will assuredly revamp our perception of processors.

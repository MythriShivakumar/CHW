THE UPSIDE TO BEING SINGLE!

Home Personal The upside to being single!

Advantages of being single!

As promised, here is part two, the more positive aspects of being single. If you are wondering why I am calling it part two, I encourage you to go read part 1, IT IS OKAY TO BE SINGLE!, wherein I talk about the various lows that follow along with being single. If you never found singlehood to be a trouble or if it never saddened you (don’t lie), then you would probably not relate to that. But, this blog, you will definitely relate to because it holds almost all the positive features that complete a single life. And, if you are someone like me who has those days where they wish they were in a relationship and other days where they are grateful for being single, you will relate to both! So, anyway it is a win-win!

I’m sure many of us must have come across phrases like ‘You are lucky you are single’. Especially when your friend is going through a breakup and they are right at times. While you will probably end up being committed to a person, for the rest of your life, until you find that person you will find being single extremely fun for the most part. So here are a few of my favorite benefits that come along with being single!!!

#1. Pocket-Friendly

Yes, I know we are not talking about a hotel but a relationship status. But, if you do get into a relationship, you will have to look at a LOT of pocket-friendly things. When you are a student and do not have a job, you basically are broke. And whatever budget you have is not much. And paying for dates and more will eventually leave your pocket empty.

Sure, you take turns while paying and more. But you will have to step up your game when it comes to giving gifts for various occasions that stuff is not cheap. But when you are single? One less person to gift! Oh and anniversaries? Yeah..They don’t exist! And your savings? Keeps increasing!

#2. Time to invest in yourself

I know, I know. All you people in a relationship will probably start going off about how you get a lot of time and your partner values your space and all that. But, even you have to admit you will have to dedicate a certain time period in your day to them. That is important in fact, in a healthy relationship.

A relationship will require you to divide your time and invest in the other person. Because let’s be honest, it takes a lot of time and energy to sustain a healthy relationship. While a person who has no such commitments gets to do whatever that pleases them. Binge-watching, napping, reading, all the various activities, and literally no one to answer to! Which brings me to the next benefit.

#3. You are your own boss!

I absolutely hate being told what to do. In fact, I go out of my way and do the opposite. So, when you are single, you can do absolutely anything you want and no one will raise a single question! Factor in a partner and you have to not only divide your attention but your time and your actions need to be reported as well. Some days, you just want the whole day to yourself. You want to go on a wild spree and travel and more! Not saying the ones who are in a relationship don’t get to do that

But as mentioned above, a relationship requires a two-way commitment and it would indeed mean, you will have to consider the other person’s needs as well. But when you are single? You can flirt with no fear, you can wake up and go to a whole new place and not worry about your partner not being up for it. So if you want to dance to your own tune, singlehood will let you do that to the fullest!

From someone who has been single the whole while, I know these upsides make you grin ear to ear! Sure, there will be days where those cute couples on Instagram will make you feel all single. But, there will also be days where you thank all your stars for not letting your heartbreak as someone else’s did. It is not about forcing it to happen.

I have always believed in the phrase “Everything happens for a reason”, so just let the universe work things out for you. The best is yet to come, my friend. Until then, keep slaying and keep loving yourself a little more, every day!

YOU ARE NOT ALONE!

This topic is something I relate to on a personal level. It is common to have dark days. It could be due to mood swings, rejection, breakups, even a nightmare, and innumerable others. And there is no dark phase which is darker than the other.

You are NOT supposed to feel worthless. Your brain is not allowed to be your enemy. So do not ever think that you do not need help or that you are fine. You will certainly be fine but until that normalcy is achieved, it is okay to feel vulnerable. It is perfectly fine to let someone help you. And it is totally alright to admit that you are just not in the right space.

Emotions are not something that should be hidden. I understand that you may be afraid to show what you feel or you think that someone is going to take advantage and exploit your weakness. Maybe someone already has and that led to the trust issues which do not allow for you to seek help. I am sorry if that has happened to you but what you need to remember is that not everyone is the same, as cliche as it sounds.

We are conditioned to think that asking for help will portray you as a ‘weak’ person. If you still think so, it is my pleasure to burst that big bubble for you. Seeking help is the most courageous thing one can do. I must admit, I am still on the path of being courageous, which makes me the best person to tell you all about this journey you will traverse.

It is terrible if you think you do not deserve help because trust me, everyone does. As said before, you do not want your mind to wage a war with you. Do not let it push you over the ledge. You may think you are the only one who will understand you but you are just not looking beyond you.

There is always someone who wants to be there for you. Someone whose life is better because of you and someone who just wants to know you for you. Seek professional help, if advised. Or just reach out to your friends or family. And if you feel that they are not the right person? You can stop anytime! It is totally up to you. You control these reins! And the power lies in knowing when to loosen and when to pull it back.

Let me share something that may be relatable. I have always felt like talking to another person about what is upsetting me, would burden them. Unhealthy and terrible habit, I am aware. I had this mindset because I constantly encouraged all my friends to speak to me and I intended to be someone who solves their problem and helps them feel better. Which in turn made me assume that sharing would add up to their problems.

I am certain there are people out there who have felt or feel the same. Let me tell you this, YOU ARE WRONG. Your friends want you to feel just as happy as you make them feel. Again, there is always someone out there whose life is better with you in it. Do not let the loneliness seep in. How did I start opening up? It is so not easy. Initially, you will feel guilty but later you will feel this high, of happiness. It is a totally different kind of high and you deserve to feel it.

Do not let your mind trick you into thinking that you are alone, or you need to be strong for everyone else. Do not beat yourself up for your dark days. Everyone has them and you will get through it. Bottling them up will just lead to an explosion that is chaotic! Remember, you are not alone. You are loved and you will get through it.

If you feel you need a total stranger’s opinion there are various hotlines you can reach out to all throughout the day and they will help you. Because YOU MATTER.

  • Vandrevala Foundation: 1860 2662 345 / +91 730 459 9836

You can reach out to me as well! We can traverse this journey together. It will be tough, but you will certainly get through it!

Sending tons of love and happiness your way,
M
